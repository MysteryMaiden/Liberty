Experience summary

I have developed a wide range of personal and collaborative projects that demonstrate strong technical ability, creativity, and self-direction. My skills include strong proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and JSON, with additional experience in Python, GitHub version control, ServiceNow workflows, and UI building. I regularly troubleshoot technical issues through research, experimentation, and structured problem-solving, and I'm accustomed to teaching myself new software, creative techniques, and coding concepts through hands-on work.

Beyond web development, I’ve built and maintained long-term creative projects, including multiple book series, an animated YouTube show in production, and several independent passion projects that require planning, scripting, coordination, and iterative development. I have leadership experience through school programs such as Student Body Officers and the National Honor Society, where I managed events, led tutoring initiatives, and guided team-based projects like the Academic Resource Library.

Looking forward, I aim to transition into artificial intelligence and neurotechnology as an entrepreneur. I’m driven by curiosity, innovation, and a desire to build technologies that meaningfully improve people’s lives. My independent learning, project management, and technical foundation have prepared me to contribute to forward-thinking teams—and ultimately to develop groundbreaking solutions of my own.
