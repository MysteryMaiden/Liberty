Hobbies and Interests

  • Reading

    “I’m an avid reader with a strong love for fantasy and sci-fi, though I enjoy branching into mystery and general fiction as well. Stories with lore rich worlds and compelling characters draw me in the most. My current favorite is Six of Crows from the Grishaverse, mostly because of the unpredictibility within the storyline and character interactions.

  • Writing

    Writing is another major passion of mine. I’ve always loved telling stories, and I naturally weave a sense of narrative into nearly everything I do. I’m currently working on several books, scripts, and short stories; most of them in the fantasy genre, since I enjoy writing the same kinds of worlds I love to read about.

  • Art

    I enjoy art of all kinds, I do digital art, traditional art, sewing crafting, really any artsy thing under the sun. a few of my favorite art mediums are watercolor, markers digital art, and sketching, just because I'm the most familiar with them. but I'm always on the look out for a new medium to try.

  • Coding

    I enjoy lots of technology related activities, including coding. I began learning to code at a young age through block-like coding with scratch and tinker, and have evolved into using javascript, HTML, css, python, and a couple of other coding languages.
